Why Dil Se Foundation?

Education is the single most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world. At "Dil Se Education" we support programs that improve the educational lives of underprivileged children in partnership with Government Schools across the state. We believe that every child educated will lead to a better and strong society.

Who We Are

Dil Se Education is an initiative started by a group of people who believe that each and every child in the world deserves access to quality education – a chance to learn and grow in a nurturing, child-friendly and safe environment. Education is the most effective tool at the society’s disposal to build a better today and tomorrow. We at Dil Se are dedicated to using this tool efficiently to help our young citizens of the future.

Currently, we are partnering with local/state government to revamp Mandal Parishad Primary School (MPPS) in Ramachandrapuram, Hyderabad.

What We Do

Our mission is to make a profound and sustaining difference in the lives of the children of our country. At Dil Se, we initiate and support programs that improve the physical, emotional and educational lives of underprivileged and at-risk children. Education goes beyond facilitating learning, it imparts knowledge, develops skills, values and good habits. It provides underprivileged children a launching platform and helps to break out communities from the per- verse cycle of poverty and ignorance. Currently, we are partnering with local/state government to revamp Mandal Parishad Primary School (MPPS) in Ramachandrapuram, Hyderabad.
